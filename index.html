Our Chairman

I am extremely happy to learn that Shirdi Sai Public School has celebrated its seventeenth Foundation Day on 17th July 2020. The school was established in the year 2003 with only 18 students and today it has more than 1800 students. It’s rate of growth is highly appreciable. The culture and standard of education of the school is a model in Moradabad, to be followed by other schools. Besides the educational curriculum, the school aims to act upon character building. The multidimensional sports and cultural activities will help a lot in building the personality of the students to serve the new World. I congratulate the Trust, Shri Sai Karuna Sansthan, for establishing such an educational institution of excellence. I also congratulate the Trustees, the Principal and the faculty for their valuable contribution. I am convinced that this institution will reach greater heights in future. May Shri Sai Bless Us All
Shri C.B.Satpathy
